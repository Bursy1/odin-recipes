Make a Gourmet Grilled Cheese!

This is what your dish will look like by the end of this recipe!

Looking for an easy midweek breakfast option? Try this extremely simple yet delicious Gourmet Grilled Cheese Sandwich. This recipe serves 1 person.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of Italian bread
  • 1 tablespoon softened butter, divided
  • 1 tablespoon prepared pesto sauce, divided
  • 1 slice provolone cheese
  • 2 slices of tomato
  • 1 slice American cheese

Cooking Instructions

  1. Step 1

    2. Spread one side of a slice of bread with butter, and place it buttered side down, into a nonstick skillet over medium heat.

  2. Step 2

    3. Spread the top of the bread slice in the skillet with half the pesto sauve, and place a slive of provolone cheese, the tomato slices, and the slice of American cheese onto the pesto.

  3. Step 3

    4. Spread remaining pesto sauce on one side of the second slice of bread, and place the bread slice, pesto side down, onto the sandwich. Butter the top side of the sandwich.

  4. Step 4

    5. Gently fry the sandwich, flipping once, until both sides of the bread are golden brown and the cheese has melted, about 5 minutes per side.

Cook's Note

Definitely easy, definitely low effort!
